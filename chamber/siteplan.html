Website Planning Document

Site Name: Baguio Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the different organization on local businesses in the city of Baguuio.

Optional Domain Availability: https://www.facebook.com/HeartWarriorsPhInc

Site Purpose

The site will serve as the central hub for businesses in Baguio City. It provides up-to-date information on local events, facilitates networking opportunities, and maintains comprehensive business directories. By encouraging local shopping and collaboration, the site is dedicated to promoting sustainable economic growth, supporting entrepreneurs, and strengthening the city’s business community.
