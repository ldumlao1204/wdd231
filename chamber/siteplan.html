Website Planning Document

Site Name: LD's Chamber of Commerce

This name represents a non-profit organization on local businesses in the Philippines.

Optional Domain Availability: https://www.facebook.com/HeartWarriorsPhInc

Site Purpose

The site provides various businesseswareness by providing medical information, both to parents and patients, along with a membership information form to each designated area. It also seeks donors and benefactors who will give financial and moral support to families living with congenital heart disease patients. It promotes love and hope within a community of people fighting with these diseases.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find more information about this group?
