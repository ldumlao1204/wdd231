Understanding Tetralogy of Fallot

❤

What is ToF?

Tetralogy of Fallot is a congenital heart condition made up of four related defects that affect the heart's structure and blood flow. It is the most common cyanotic heart defect.

⚕

Treatment & Surgery

Most children with ToF undergo open-heart surgery within the first year of life. The surgery repairs the heart defects, allowing normal blood flow and improved oxygen levels.

🌟

Living with ToF

With advances in surgical techniques, most people with repaired ToF lead full, active lives. Regular cardiology follow-ups ensure long-term heart health and early intervention if needed.
